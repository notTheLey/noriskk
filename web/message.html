Deathcraft Ethical Community Agreement (DECA)

Summary

To ensure that you fully understand and agree to the terms, you must carefully read the entire DECA. However, here is a brief summary of the most important points:

  • Behavior: Insulting and inappropriate behavior is prohibited, as is sharing personal data.
  • Privacy: Recording, editing, or sharing audio or video content is reserved for our partners and team members.
  • Legal: Your buildings and creative ideas are entirely your property, but they may be fully used or shared by us and our partners.
  • Submissions: Your submissions remain your property; however, we gain usage rights to the ideas and content that you submit.
  • Data Collection: We collect data regarding your activities, behavior, messages, uploads, and profiles.

1. Behavior

  • Prohibited Actions: Harassment, insulting language, and offensive actions are strictly prohibited.
  • Respect for Privacy: Do not share personal or sensitive information about others without consent.
  • What’s Allowed: Friendly and respectful interactions are encouraged, with constructive criticism in a non-offensive manner.

2. Privacy

  • Recording and Sharing Content: Only team members and partners may record, edit, or share content.
  • What’s Allowed: You can create and share your content but must respect others' privacy.
  • Why Privacy is Important: Protecting personal data is crucial to maintaining trust.

3. Legal Ownership

  • Your Creative Works: You own your creations, but we can use and share them.
  • Usage and Sharing: Deathcraft and partners may incorporate your work into projects.
  • What You Can’t Do: You cannot prevent us from using your creations or copy our content without permission.

4. Submissions

  • Ownership of Submissions: Your submissions remain yours, but we gain rights to use and distribute them.
  • What’s Allowed: Submit original ideas, but understand we can use them freely.

5. Data Collection

  • What Data We Collect: Activity, behavior, messages, uploads, and profile information.
  • What We Do With the Data: Data is used to improve the community experience and for administrative purposes.
  • Data Protection: We commit to storing your data securely and respecting your privacy.

6. General Guidelines

  • Respect for the Community: Maintain respect and cooperation to foster a collaborative environment.
  • Enforcement and Consequences: Violations can result in penalties, from warnings to bans.
  • Transparency: Changes to the DECA will be communicated clearly.

7. Additional Notes

  • Informed Consent: By joining, you agree to the DECA and its terms.
  • Dispute Resolution: Resolve conflicts peacefully, with moderators assisting if needed.
  • Community Support: Help each other and share ideas to strengthen the community.
